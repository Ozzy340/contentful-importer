<FAQ module – accordion> Module: FAQs List
   * <h2> Mailing and Shipping FAQ
   * <accordion>
   * <question> What is a mailing system?
<answer> Mailing systems use hardware and software to automate the process of preparing, weighing, preparing postage, and sending business mail and packages. 
Modern mailing systems, including Quadient's, go beyond the basic postage meter to integrate digital scales, address verification, carrier rate comparison, and postage accounting. That kind of mailroom automation minimizes or eliminates manual handling to increase efficiency and accuracy and reduce waste.
   * <question> How can Quadient help you mitigate the effects of rising postage costs?
<answer> Quadient mailing systems keep postage costs under consistent control by ensuring you get the best price for the right postal class automatically, and opening up access to discounted rates.
Rate Wizard and integrated scales ensure accurate postal class selection. Quadient's S.M.A.R.T. Essential Mailing Software allows organizations to compare to-the-moment rates across USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL, so you can optimize every shipment with the most cost-effective carrier and service.
   * <question> Can Quadient support both SMB and enterprise mailrooms?
<answer> Quadient mailing systems are designed to scale across both SMB and enterprise environments, from the iX-5 series, ideal for mid-level mail, to the high-output iX-9 series with all-in-one mail center software.
Whether you're operating a single location or running a high-volume mail center, Quadient has a mailing and shipping solution that's configured for your needs.
   * <question> Does Quadient offer both print and digital mail solutions?
<answer> Quadient mailing systems are part of a powerful business automation platform that connects physical mail processes with digital communication channels and finance workflows.
This unified approach allows organizations to manage customer connections on every level from a single platform, turning fragmented systems and operational complexity into simple, streamlined efficiency.
