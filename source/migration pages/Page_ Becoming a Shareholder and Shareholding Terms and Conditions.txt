<Heading> Pure registered shares 
<Content copy> Shares are held directly in a registered account linked to Quadient. 
<Heading> Administered registered shares 
<Content copy> Shares remain registered with Quadient but are managed through an authorized intermediary. 
<Heading> Bearer shares 
<Content copy> Shares are held and administered entirely through an authorized financial intermediary.
