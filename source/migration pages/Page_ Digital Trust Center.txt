Module: FAQs List
 <Heading> Common trust center questions
<SubHeading> Make due diligence faster and clearer.
<question> What does this page cover?
<Content copy> This page covers Quadient Digital software solutions and the security, privacy and compliance information associated with them. It should clearly state that other Quadient areas, including Mail, Lockers and internal IT topics, may follow different standards or support paths.
<question> Do all certifications apply to every covered solution?
<Content copy> No. The page should state this plainly and make solution-level applicability easy to understand through the certification display and related solution sections. This reduces confusion for procurement, legal and security reviewers.
<question> Which Quadient solutions are included here?
<Content copy> The page should cover Customer Communications Management, Intelligent Document Automation, Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Quadient Digital Platform, iForms and Managed Services. Each covered area should have concise context so reviewers understand what is in scope.
<question> How can I request security or privacy information?
<Content copy> Visitors should be able to contact the right team without searching. Keep both direct email routes and a broader contact path visible so questions can move quickly to privacy, security or commercial stakeholders.
<question> Where can I find privacy and legal information?
<Content copy> The page should point visitors to Quadient’s website privacy statement and terms resources in a clearly labeled related-resources section. This helps legal and procurement teams complete reviews faster.
<question> Why keep the updates archive visible?
<Content copy> A visible archive shows ongoing program maintenance and makes it easier for customers and partners to confirm recent certifications, assessments and notices. It also reinforces transparency, which is a core purpose of the current page.
